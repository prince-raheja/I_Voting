NOTICE

Hostel / Gymkhana Election (2016-17)

As per the request from students, following rules are being put forward for conducting Hostel / Gymkhana elections (2016-17). The existing members of the student body shall continue their term till the end of the current semester, while the newly elected body’s term will start immediately following the expiration of the term of the current body.

RULES-

One person can contest for one post only.

Candidate should have minimum CPI of 6.0 for contesting election. Those students who faced disciplinary action from the institute will be debarred from contesting election.

For each post, interested candidates should fill in the nomination form and their names must be seconded by 2 students.

The students who have seconded the names of a candidate for a post can not second the names of another candidate for the same post.

Students must bring their ID cards at the time of nomination and also at the time of voting.

Candidates are not allowed to make posters for canvassing and must maintain discipline on the campus. Candidate will not resort to any illegal means or insult fellow candidate in any manner.

The elections to the posts of Vice President, General Secretary – Sports, General Secretary – Cultural Affairs, General Secretary – Student Welfare & Technical Affairs and two Postgraduate Representative (one for M.Sc/M.Tech and one for Ph.D) (who are elected by the elected secretaries of different batches), will be held in the next semester once the secretaries of incoming first year students have been elected.
